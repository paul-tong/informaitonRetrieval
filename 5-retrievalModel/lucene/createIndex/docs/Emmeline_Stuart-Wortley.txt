lady emmeline charlotte elizabeth stuart-wortley ne manners 1806 20 october 1855 was an english poet and writer best known for her travels in the united states etc during 1849 and 1850 she was editor of the keepsake in 1837 and 1840 she was a daughter of john manners 5th duke of rutland and his wife elizabeth manners duchess of rutland and on 17 february 1831 married hon charles stuart-wortley-mackenzie a son of james stuart-wortley-mackenzie 1st baron wharncliffe they had a daughter lady victoria welby after the death of her husband in 1844 she began to travel as a wealthy victorian widow along with her daughter victoria in october 1855 she died of dysentery in the area between antioch and beirut while travelling through ottoman empire regions following the history of
