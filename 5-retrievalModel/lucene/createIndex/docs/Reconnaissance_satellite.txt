or intelligence applications the first generation type ie corona and zenit took photographs then ejected canisters of photographic film which would descend to earth corona capsules were retrieved in mid-air as they floated down on parachutes later spacecraft had digital imaging systems and downloaded the images via encrypted radio links in the united states most information available is on
