diogo dias also known as diogo gomes was a 15th-century portuguese explorer he was the brother of bartolomeu dias and discovered some of the cape verde islands together with antnio noli serving with da gama in 1497 on the first portuguese india armadas expedition to india diogo dias served as escrivo clerk aboard vasco da gamas flagship so gabriel dias was one of the main conduits between gama and the zamorin of calicut and was briefly taken prisoner by the zamorin when negotiations became contentious in 1500 diogo dias accompanied the 2nd armada of pedro lvares cabral as one of the captains of the fleet with a commission to open trade at sofala diogo dias was one of the first to go ashore in the discovery of brazil in april 1500 famously dias is credited for breaking the ice with the wary tupiniquim on the beach by jumping into an impromptu joyful dance to the accompaniment of tupi pipes new waters during an expedition along the cape of good hope diogo may have originally called it the cape of storms with pedro lvares cabral and his brother diass ship got separated from the main fleet during the crossing of cape of good hope his brothers ship was lost during that crossing having struck a route too far east dias was the first european to sight the island of madagascar and is often credited with naming the island of so loureno on account of it being found on st lawrences day august 10 1500 although the island was not unknown its existence and arabic name island of the moon was already reported by pro da covilh back in 1490 diass subsequent attempts to find in the area battered by tempests attacked by pirates and finally forced aground on the eritrean coast in a desperate search for water and food for his rapidly dying crew by the time he left there dias
