human outposts are artificially-created controlled human habitats located in environments inhospitable for humans such as on the ocean floor in space or on another planet the logistics and difficulties inherent in such ventures have been heavily explored in science fiction in fiction human outposts in other worlds are a common motif in science fiction whether established and occupied
